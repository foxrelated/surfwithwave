Infolinks

Infolinks allows you to integrate its highly engaging ad units into your pages with “content intent” intelligence which earns you revenue with every click.

How do I earn money with Infolinks In-Text Ads?

Earn money with Infolinks intelligent ads on your website! The Infolinks application allows you to easily integrate unobtrusive Pay Per Click ads on your pages. Infolinks’ advertising platform functions via our smart algorithm to scan your pages of content and transforms your highest paying keywords into engaging and relevant ads. Our In-Text advertising links, Related Tags and Tag Cloud capture your audience while they’re reading your own words.

Pay Per Click In-Text advertising inserts text link advertisements within the content of your website, usually in the form of double-underline hyperlinks. Upon hovering with your mouse, a floating informational bubble opens with content from an advertiser. If clicked, the visitor is directed to the advertiser's landing page and you earn advertising revenue; otherwise, when the mouse is moved away from the hyperlink, the bubble disappears.

Start earning PPC In-Text Advertising revenue!

Earning money with Infolinks is one-click simple via Wave Telecom. All you have to do is turn on Infolinks in your Wave Telecom dashboard.

From there, your Infolinks account is automatically created. Within two business days, your domain will be reviewed by the Infolinks team. You'll receive an email from Infolinks with notice of approval or decline.

Once approved, the Infolinks code will be automatically added to your website, and you'll start earning revenue right away. You do not need to do anything else.

Note: you may enable Infolinks for any number of domains in your Wave Telecom account.

Admission Terms
