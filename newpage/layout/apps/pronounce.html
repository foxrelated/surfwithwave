Pronounce

Select a word or sentence on your site and click the Pronounce icon that pops-up to hear how the selected text is pronounced.

Pronounce makes it easy for website owners to make available to their users a simple, zero configuration pronunciation tool that lets them hear how a word is pronounced without having to leave the page.

Pronounce is intelligent enough to detect the language of the page based on certain standard attributes in your markup.

Note: The service is provided as is and uses Microsoft’s Translation Tools. Wave Telecom makes no guarantee on the quality or availability of the service.
