Highlight

Highlight is a contextual search service that allows users to explore content from all over the web without leaving your website.

Highlight Demo

Watch this short video to see Highlight in action.


Contextual search

When a visitor highlights a word or phrase on your website, they are presented with a tooltip to signal that more information is available. By engaging with the tooltip, the visitor triggers the display of results based on their selected text. There's no interference with links or normal copy-paste.

Features

Web search from Bing™

Highlight presents web search results from Bing for the highlighted word or phrase. Each short description links to the full result in a new browser tab.

Definitions from DuckDuckGo

When possible, Highlight includes English definitions of the word or phrase. The definition is linked to the DuckDuckGo results for more background and sourcing.

Pronounce

We've integrated Pronounce, a separate Wave Telecom app which uses your visitor's text-to-speech engine to say the highlighted word or phrase out loud.

When you turn on Highlight for your visitors, Pronounce is enabled, too.
