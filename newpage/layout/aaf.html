Free, Pro & Business customers enjoy:
We never charge for bandwidth

Wave Telecom will never bill you for bandwidth usage. We believe if your site suddenly gets popular or suffers an attack, you shouldn't have to dread your bandwidth bill.

Mix plan types

Within one account, you can have Free, Pro and Business websites. We make it easy to upgrade or downgrade on a per site basis.

Upgrade / downgrade anytime

You can decide to upgrade to Pro or Business anytime throughout the month and get access to the features instantaneously. Or if you want to downgrade, we make it simple.

Consolidated billing

With Wave Telecom, you only get charged once per month. We group all of your sites and services on one, easy-to-understand invoice.
