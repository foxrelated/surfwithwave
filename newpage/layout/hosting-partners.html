Wave Telecom partners

Wave Telecom works regardless of what hosting provider a website is running on. However, some hosting providers have taken the extra steps to make sure their customers get the most out of Wave Telecom and have become a Wave Telecom Partner. Wave Telecom has two partner programs: Optimized Partner and Certified Partner.

Wave Telecom Optimized Partners offer all the benefits of a Wave Telecom Certified Partner, plus Railgun. Railgun ensures that the connection between an origin server and the Wave Telecom network is as fast as possible.
