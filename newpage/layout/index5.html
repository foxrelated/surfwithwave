No configuration necessary

You do not have to spend time designating which files to cache. Wave Telecom’s technology builds the cache for you based on your traffic and what objects on your site are safe to serve from the edge.

Works with static and dynamic content

Wave Telecom caches static resources including JavaScript, CSS and images. Your dynamic content continues to be served direct from your web server automatically. You can choose which subdomains that you want to be a part of Wave Telecom’s CDN and which ones that you do not.

Page Rules

Page Rules provide you with fine-grained control of Wave Telecom on each page of your site. You can enable full HTML caching, individually customize Wave Telecom’s features on a page by page basis or enable edge network forwarding to improve page delivery even further.

Always Online™

If your server ever goes offline, Wave Telecom will serve a limited copy of your cached website to keep it online for your visitors. Wave Telecom builds the Always Online™ version of your website, so your most popular pages are represented.

Protect against traffic surges

If you get a surge in traffic, Wave Telecom’s CDN will absorb the effect so your server stays online. On average, you'll see 65% fewer requests to your servers and save 60% of your bandwidth, even with Wave Telecom's free plan.

Automatic IPv6

Make your site available to visitors on IPv6 networks with the click of a button. No need to rush to upgrade your infrastructure. Keep your IPv4 infrastructure until you're ready, we'll make it seem to the world you're running a modern dual-stack.

Rock solid reliability

With Wave Telecom’s distributed network, if there is an issue in one of the data centers, visitors to the other data centers are unaffected. If the issue persists, our system can easily reroute visitors to the next closest data center. The end result is a seamless experience for your visitors. Check out our network map to learn more. For more information about Wave Telecom's reliability and uptime guarantees, visit our Business and Enterprise page.

Works with other CDNs

If you’re already using a CDN, Wave Telecom works as a complement. You have the choice of routing CDN traffic through Wave Telecom first (saving you bandwidth & money) or bypassing Wave Telecom altogether for your CDN traffic.

How can Wave Telecom offer a free service?

Wave Telecom believes that every website should be fast. Because of Wave Telecom’s technology, we’ve been able to build a CDN that is available to any website online. From the beginning, we worked to minimize the costs of serving every byte. Wherever possible, we use our own custom-built systems that are designed for this purpose. We buy our bandwidth through the wholesale market, which means we're paying for the size of our pipe, not for each byte we serve through it. We also peer with other networks wherever possible in order to drive the cost of bandwidth as close to zero as possible. We never charge for bandwidth and are committed to always offering a FREE version of Wave Telecom with at least today’s feature set. Wave Telecom offers additional plans and services which offer even better services at reasonable prices. We hope you love the service and choose to upgrade.

Pro features

Session optimization

Wave Telecom’s pre-fetching will automatically identify and then optimize the delivery of your most requested resources. Your most popular assets get preloaded into your visitor’s browser so that your second, third and fourth page loads are extra fast.

Prioritized requests

No matter how heavy your server load is, your traffic will never slow down. Wave Telecom prioritizes requests for Pro customers' websites for maximum performance.

SPDY

SPDY is a new protocol that removes the bottlenecks of HTTP. You can support SPDY without having to make any changes to your origin server. Visitors with browsers that support SPDY connect to Wave Telecom over the protocol. Wave Telecom handles the multiplexing and begins sending down objects that we have in cache. The request to the origin server for non-cached objects is sent over standard HTTP/S. Get SPDY support with a single click.

Business and Enterprise features

Railgun™

Railgun helps eliminate the speed-of-light latency and cache the uncacheable by using a combination of compression techniques to achieve, on average, 99.6% compression of previously non-cached web objects. This results in a 730% performance increase on average, for web traffic from a web server to Wave Telecom's network.

Truly get the most out of your network. Learn more about Railgun.
